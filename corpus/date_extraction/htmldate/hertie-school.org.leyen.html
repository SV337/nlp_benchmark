What's on the cards for von der Leyen?

Markus Jachtenfuchs talks on German radio about the challenges ahead for the new European Commission.

After vetoing three Commissioner candidates in November, the European Parliament has confirmed the new European Commission of President Ursula von der Leyen. Markus Jachtenfuchs, Professor of European and Global Governance, spoke to Radio Eins about the challenges for the new Commission including climate policy, the EU's role in NATO and its standing in the international world order.

Listen to the full interview (in German) on Radio Eins.

The school offers masterâs programmes, executive education and doctoral programmes, distinguished by interdisciplinary and practice-oriented teaching, as well as outstanding research. Its extensive international network positions it as an ambassador of good governance, characterised by public debate and engagement.
