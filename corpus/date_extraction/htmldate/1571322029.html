Profile[edit]

  • Name: 박예진 / Park Ye Jin (Bak Yeh Jin)
  • Profession: Actress and singer
  • Birthdate: 1981-Apr-01 (age 39)
  • Birthplace: Seoul, South Korea
  • Height: 165cm
  • Weight: 48kg
  • Star sign: Aries
  • Blood type: B
  • Family: Sister and husband/actor Park Hee Soon
  • Talent agency: Saram Entertainment
