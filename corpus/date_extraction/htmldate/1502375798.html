      NEW DELHI: Shares of PSU banks were trading with gains in Thursday's morning session.

      Shares of Central Bank of India (up 1.89 per cent), Canara Bank (up 1.28 per cent), UCO Bank (up 1.25 per cent), J & K Bank (up 1.21 per cent), Punjab National Bank (up 0.82 per cent), Bank of Baroda (up 0.8 per cent), Union Bank of India (up 0.68 per cent), State Bank of India (up 0.49 per cent), Indian Overseas Bank (up 0.44 per cent), Indian Bank (up 0.43 per cent) and Punjab & Sind Bank (up 0.24 per cent) were among the top gainers.

      The Nifty PSU Bank index was trading 0.68 per cent up at 2426.3 around 10:38 am.

      Benchmark NSE Nifty50 index was up 41.30 points at 12148.2 , while the BSE Sensex was up 152.71 points at 41268.09 .

      Among the 50 stocks in the Nifty index, 31 were trading in the green, while 19 were in the red.

      Shares of Vodafone Idea, YES Bank, Indian Oil Corp, Zee Ent., Tata Motors, RBL Bank Ltd., Ibull HousingFin, Axis Bank, SBI and PFC were among the most traded shares on the NSE.
